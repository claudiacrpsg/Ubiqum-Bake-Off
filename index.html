About

Ubiqum Bake Off is a baking competition. It was created as the students of Ubiqum Code Academy Java Web Development 116 Course realized that, apart from their love of coding, all of them also loved baking!

To find out who was the best baker, this challenge was set up with the help of the four Ubiqum's Web Development mentors, who are the judges in this competition.

In this first edition of Ubiqum Bake Off there are 14 bakers competing along 4 challenges: Cupcakes, Biscuits, Cakes and Pies. For each challenge, the mentors will score the bakers from 0 to 10.

Bake Off Rules

1. The bakers have to be students of Ubiqum in order to participate.

2. The bakers have to be able to commit to the dates of the 4 challenges.

3. The bakers can bring written aids to help guide them through the competition.
